Empowering Teens to Embrace Their Unedited, Unfiltered Selves Re-branding Here.Now. as a new premier and comprehensive platform that educates, strengthens and empowers teens to confront mental health challenges as a community.


WHY? Inspired by Instagram influencer Essena O’neill who let her 612K followers see behind the curtain of her curation when she realized how much work it took to keep up with appearances, we asked ourselves whether this was an unspoken truth among teens facing mental health challenges.

What we found was that for many teens, their self-esteem depends on the amount of selfies, likes, shares, tweets and snaps from friends. Moreover, teens often compare their true, imperfect lives to filtered and perfectly edited snapshots of celebrities and their peers, altering their self perceptions, and ultimately making them vulnerable to mental health issues.

In order to cultivate an inclusive, dynamic and empowering community, we re-branded Here.Now. and developed a digital integrated platform that meets teens where they live online and off-line. Our process – roll out a 12-month, three-phase plan that educates teens about mental health with access to comprehensive resources and information, strengthens the positive perceptions teens have of themselves, and empowers teens to show their true selves, especially on social media.

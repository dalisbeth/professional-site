Hello, Boston! Easing the moving process for General Electric employees and celebrating their homecoming with the Boston community.


WHY? General Electric’s relocation to Boston has made many people, both employees and the Boston community, uneasy. With so many life altering changes being made, GE will need to maintain the trust of their employees by ensuring that they are informed every step of the way. GE will also need to address their plan’s positive impact on the greater Boston community to offset the financial implications of the move.

In addition to providing "every step of the way" resources to help GE's Boston employees get settled and adapt to a new environment, we positioned the relocation as a homecoming celebration, in order to create opportunities for employees and the community to build positive relationships with one another. Who doesn't enjoy a good housewarming! HOW?
